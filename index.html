About

 

For the last 2 years I have worn many hats while working for Robert Stephen Consulting. Hired as an IWMS Administrator, I quickly transitioned to a CAD Drafter/Specialist position within a few months after our lead CAD technician moved across the country with his family.


 

Around 1 year on the job I grew into a front end web development role creating Bootstrap web pages with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript & jQuery, and have really fallen in love with web design and development ever since. I’ve been able to get my hands on technologies such as AutoCAD, Revit, ARCHIBUS, SpaceView, Jira, Crystal Reports and numerous others that I hadn't heard of prior to my experience here.


 

Currently working towards earning the JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures Certification from freeCodeCamp.
