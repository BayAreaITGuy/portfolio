Monterey Bay Aquarium

Animal Encounters: This unique experience is offered on a very limited basis dependent on certain criteria and the business need, such as:

- Cultivating a donor (Development) or client relationship (SFW, C&S, Event Sales)

- Service recovery (Sales, GE)

- Certain external colleagues visiting on business (Requires manager approval)

- Political or entertainment celebrities

- Other business needs

Animal encounters must be requested at least two days in advance. After your request has been processed, you will be contacted with the details. Please note- If your request is less than 2 days, please email the husbandry contact list.

Private Tours: Some animal encounter requests require private tours (i.e. high level donors, VIP guests, celebrities). The Programs Team will do their best to accommodate these requests with at least one week notice.
